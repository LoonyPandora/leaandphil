Directions to St Peter's Church from A69 West Bound

Having joined the A69 travel for approximately 10 minutes (from Newcastle), then turn left when signposted for Stocksfield A6309; follow the road for approximately 5 minutes. You will eventually reach a cross junction (if you reach a bridge you have gone too far). At this junction go straight ahead for Bywell. Follow the road to the end where you will reach St Peters Church.

Directions to St Peter's Church from A69 East Bound

Travelling on the A69 East bound you will reach a roundabout signposted for Corbridge. Take the 3rd exit signposted for A68 Darlington. At the next roundabout take the first exit signposted for Stocksfield. Travel along the A695 for 5 minutes until you reach an S-bend signposted Stocksfield. Take the first left signposted Bywell and follow the road over a bridge bearing to the left until you reach a cross junction. Take an immediate left and follow the road to the end where you will reach St Peters Church.

As snowmen are a familiar sight in Bywell, and the church is down a small country lane, guests may wish to wrap up warm and ladies bring suitable additional footwear in case of snowfall.

St Peter’s Church, Bywell, Stocksfield, Northumberland, NE43 7AD

Directions from St Peter’s Church to Close House

From St Peter’s Church drive to the end of the country lane and turn right. Travel approximately 500 yards and turn left before the bridge, signposted Ovingham/Wylam. Follow the road until you reach a T junction and turn right, signposted Ovingham/Wylam. Follow the road through the village signposted Wylam, eventually reaching Wylam where you should follow signposts for the A69. At the next junction turn right, signposted Heddon on the Wall. You will soon see a sign for Close House. Ignore this sign and continue driving until you reach the main entrance gates for Close House on your right.
